About Me

My Life Story | A Snapshot

In the year 2000, I was born in Auckland, New Zealand. I spent my early childhood however in Bielefeld, Germany and South Carolina. Moving around a lot meant that family would be one of my few constants. To this day I treasure the familial values of sacrifice and love that were taught to me from a young age.

Ever since I can remember I've loved saving my money and finding ways to use it better. After my family began a small investing business in 2016, I've been fascinated with finance and business operations. I hope to pursue a Masters of Accountancy from Brigham Young University, and later pursue a career in Management Consulting.

Perhaps my biggest focus right now, however, is preparing to marry the love of my life this summer. I met my fiancee, Katherine, through a blind date, and we quickly fell in love. From cooking and cleaning, to planning trips and adventures, I can't wait to really start my life with the woman of my dreams.
